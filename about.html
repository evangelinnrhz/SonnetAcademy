About Sonnet Academy

Know our story, our people, and the values that guide us.

Our History

Academia de Sonneta (Sonnet Academy) was established in September 9, 1989, with a vision to provide holistic, quality education that nurtures every student's intellectual, moral, and social development. From humble beginnings, our institution has grown into a trusted center of learning in the community.

Over the years, we have expanded our academic programs, welcomed thousands of graduates, and continuously raised the bar for excellence in education. Our rich history is a testament to the dedication of our founders, faculty, and the generations of learners who have passed through our halls.

Today, Sonnet Academy stands as a beacon of educational excellence — rooted in tradition, but ever forward-looking.

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To envision a generation of learners who pursue knowledge not merely for utility, but for wisdom; who cultivate passion alongside intellect; and who contribute to society through creativity, virtue, and purposeful leadership.

Our Mission

To nurture the mind, heart, and spirit through a holistic education rooted in intellectual curiosity, artistic expression, moral integrity, and the courageous pursuit of one's calling. We inspire students to seek truth, embrace excellence, and transform their passions into meaningful contributions to the world.

Our Core Values

Passion · Creativity · Compassion · Excellence — guiding everything we do inside and outside the halls.
