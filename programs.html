Programs Overview

Sonnet Academy offers a comprehensive range of academic programs tailored to different learning stages. Each program is designed to develop well-rounded individuals equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in today's world.

Academic Levels

Kinder Program

Ages 5–6

A nurturing foundation that fosters early childhood development through play-based learning, social interaction, and creative exploration in a safe and supportive environment.

  • Play-based curriculum
  • Language readiness
  • Numeracy foundations
  • Values formation

Elementary Program

Grades 1–6

Building strong academic foundations in core subjects while encouraging curiosity, critical thinking, and character development across six formative years of primary education.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Science & Mathematics
  • Filipino & English literacy
  • Arts & P.E. integration

Junior High School

Grades 7–10

Preparing students for higher academic challenges through an enriched curriculum that deepens subject knowledge and develops research, analytical, and leadership skills.

  • Enriched core subjects
  • Research & inquiry skills
  • Student leadership
  • Tech & digital literacy

Senior High School

Grades 11–12

A specialized two-year program aligned with the K–12 curriculum, offering academic and technical-vocational tracks designed to prepare students for college, employment, or entrepreneurship.

  • STEM / ABM / HUMSS tracks
  • Work immersion
  • College entrance prep
  • Career guidance

Senior High School Tracks

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

Ideal for students pursuing careers in medicine, engineering, computer science, and research.

ABM

Accountancy, Business & Management

Prepares students for business, finance, economics, and entrepreneurship pathways.

HUMSS

Humanities & Social Sciences

For students inclined toward law, journalism, education, political science, and social work.

TVL

Technical-Vocational Livelihood

Equips students with practical skills and TESDA certifications for immediate employment.
